About

Established in 1963, Small Dimensions Daycare & Pre-School started as a small, family owned in-home center and then grew into a fully licensed child care center serving Bellevue, Washington area families for over 50 years. We're still family owned, and have maintained the same level of personalized, quality care and learning for children ages one through five.

Services

Our instructors are bilingual English/Spanish.

We teach the children sign language to launch verbal development.

We offer small teacher to child ratios.

Our program is Montessori and Reggio inspired.

We believe children learn best through play.

Staff

Our caring, experienced staff is dedicated to meeting the individual needs of the children we care for. They're each highly qualified, making sure that your child thrives in our educational and playful environment, always putting your child's safety first.
